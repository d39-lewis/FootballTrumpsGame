Gameplay

1

Deciding who goes first

The starting player is decided randomly at the beginning of each game. Each player receives a deck of 15 cards shuffled and ready to go.

2

Playing your turn

The top card from your deck is revealed. You must choose one of the six attributes — Finishing, Defending, Sprinting, Strength, Tech or Leadership — that you want to compare against your opponent's card.

3

Winning a round

If your chosen attribute is higher than your opponent's, you win the round. You collect your opponent's card and both cards are placed at the bottom of your deck. You then stay in control and pick the attribute for the next card.

4

Losing a round

If your attribute is lower, your card goes to your opponent and control passes to them. Your opponent (the bot) will now be choosing the attribute to compare on their next turn.

5

Winning the game

The game ends when one player holds all 30 cards in their deck. That player is declared the winner!

🏆

Win = +100 points. Points can be spent in the store to buy card packs and grow your collection. Losses do not award any points, so choose your attributes wisely!
